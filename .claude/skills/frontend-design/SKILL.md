# Narada Frontend Design

> Background knowledge for all frontend work. Ensures every session produces consistent, distinctive UI matching Narada's dark editorial aesthetic.

## Audience

Savvy DJs, tastemakers, crate-diggers curating playlists for friends. They care about music deeply and expect a tool that reflects that care.

## Aesthetic: Dark Editorial Music

Dim-lit record shop meets design magazine. Dark-mode primary — the default experience, not an afterthought. Album art glows against warm dark surfaces. Data presented with editorial confidence.

## Anti-Patterns (NEVER do these)

- **Fonts**: Inter, Roboto, Open Sans, Lato, Arial, system fonts for display text
- **Colors**: Purple gradients, cold gray surfaces, pure white backgrounds, blue-purple accents
- **Layout**: Generic dashboard cards, cookie-cutter grids, predictable component patterns
- **Palette**: Timid, evenly-distributed colors. Dominant accent + restraint > pastels everywhere

## Typography

| Role | Font | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| Display/headings | **Space Grotesk** | Extreme weight contrast: 200 vs 700+. Size jumps 3x+ between levels. |
| Body/reading | **Newsreader** | Editorial serif. Warmth that sans-serif alone can't provide. |
| Mono/technical | **JetBrains Mono** | Workflow JSON, ISRCs, code. |

## Color

- **Dark surfaces**: warm-tinted near-black `oklch(0.13 0.01 60)`, not cold gray
- **Primary accent**: warm gold `oklch(0.75 0.15 85)` — sharp against dark
- **Secondary accent**: soft coral `oklch(0.70 0.12 25)`
- **Connector colors**: Spotify green, Last.fm red, Apple pink — identification only, sparingly
- Album art is a color source — let it breathe and glow

## Motion

- CSS-first for simple transitions (150ms interactive, 300ms layout)
- Motion library for orchestrated sequences
- **Page load**: staggered reveal with `animation-delay`
- One well-orchestrated moment > scattered micro-interactions

## Components

- **shadcn/ui** foundation in `web/src/components/ui/` — owned source, Radix primitives
- Customize to warm dark editorial aesthetic — default shadcn/ui themes are too neutral
- `AlbumArt` component: warm glow against dark canvas, blur for contextual backgrounds

## Key Principle

> This should feel like a tool built by someone who cares about music, not generated by an AI that doesn't know what a record sounds like.
